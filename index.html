Our Story

25

Years ago, Seppo Ainamo was living in Harare when he met a young boy who was quite the flying acrobat. After a short conversation, Seppo learned that the young man’s family couldn’t afford to send him to school.

Before long, Uncle Seppo stepped in to offer some asistance. A simple beginning to a long and impactful journey.

Since that day, Dzikwa Trust has provided over 1 Million free meals and nurtured 1,000s of vulnerable students.
